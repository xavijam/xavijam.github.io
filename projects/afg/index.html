Antarctic Field Guides

Customizable field guides for Antarctic travelers

2010

When I started at Vizzuality, I first learnt the projects we develop will help researching scientific world and environment and this project could be a perfect example.

With this web application we could explore Antarctica, all its biodiversity, the research stations,... and of course met my favourite animals, Gentoo penguins. In a month project, we helped scientist and amateurs their preparation for a trip to this wonderful continent. We develop our own tiles and we use them over OpenLayers with a specific projection. All of this for rendering properly the features in the map.

Also we transformed our taxonomy browser developed in Flash (http://www.mountainbiodiversity.org/tool.html) to JavaScript (http://afg.scarmarbin.be/species).
