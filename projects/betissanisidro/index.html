Betis San Isidro

Helping my childhood football team

2010

When I was young I played in an amateur football team, called Betis San Isidro. They were in trouble after a bad management, and when they asked me for help I didn't have any doubt.

This was my first serious experience with PHP (I still remember our first PHP project in Vizzuality, Runnity, mentioned in Google Maps Mania) and Wordpress.

I designed and developed a specific layout/theme for the team (available in my GitHub account), did the maintenance of the website, updated every week the team info and posted some news :D.

Unfortunately the adventure finished in 2012 when a new board arrived to the team and decided to use resources paid.
